### Lesson: BIP32 Paths

- Standard for *derivation paths* in HD Wallets
- Notation
  - `m/44'/0'/0'/0/0`
  - `[0x8000002c, 0x80000000, 0x80000000, 0, 0]`
- BIP44 / BIP49 / BIP84

----

### Lesson: BIP32 Paths

- BIP44
  - `m/44'/0'/0'/0/0`
  - `m/purpose'/coin'/account'/change/index`

----
